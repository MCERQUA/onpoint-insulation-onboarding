Eco-Friendly Practices

We've developed a strategy to authentically communicate your environmental commitment without greenwashing.

Authentic Environmental Commitment

🌱 Honest Approach to Sustainability

Acknowledging that spray foam installation isn't inherently eco-friendly but focusing on real practices to reduce environmental impact.

🔌 Shore Power Utilization

Using electrical connections at job sites to reduce reliance on diesel generators, resulting in quieter, cleaner job sites.

♻️ Waste Reduction Strategies

Employing skilled technicians with "On-Point" precision application to reduce overspray and trim waste.

🍃 Low-VOC Materials

Using spray foam with lower volatile organic compound content for health and environmental benefits.

Communication Strategy

🔎 Transparency About Industry Realities

Acknowledging the environmental challenges of construction and insulation while avoiding misleading "green" claims.

📊 Focus on Measurable Benefits

Quantifying energy savings, reduced carbon footprint, and waste reduction compared to industry standards.

🎓 Education About Long-Term Benefits

Explaining how energy-efficient homes reduce lifetime environmental impact and the environmental cost/benefit analysis.

📸 Visual Demonstration

Before/after photos of clean job sites, images showing reusable vs. disposable materials, and documentation of precision techniques.

Energy Efficiency & Home Performance

We've developed a strategy to position OnPoint Insulation as experts in whole-home performance, not just insulation.

Comprehensive Air Sealing Packages

Standard Air Sealing Package

  • Targeted air sealing of major penetrations
  • Weather stripping for doors and windows
  • Basic attic air sealing
  • Visual inspection and manual detection

Advanced Air Sealing Package

  • Comprehensive air sealing of all penetrations
  • Detailed rim joist and sill sealing
  • Includes blower door directed air sealing
  • Thermal imaging to identify problem areas
  • Before/after blower door testing

Whole-Home Performance Package

  • Complete building envelope assessment
  • Strategic insulation upgrade recommendations
  • Moisture and ventilation analysis
  • Energy modeling and savings projections
